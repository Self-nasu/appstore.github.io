Introduction and Purpose

What is the main purpose of Editor Choice?

Editor Choice is a platform designed to cater to the needs of editors in the mobile and PC community. Our main purpose is to provide a centralised platform where editors can stay updated with the latest editing apps available in the market. We curate a comprehensive list of the most widely used apps by professional editors, making it convenient for new editors to find and access the best tools without any hassle. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced editor, Editor Choice strives to be your go-to resource for discovering and exploring the top editing apps in the industry.

Naman is a computer science student with a passion for various creative pursuits, including blender animation, graphic design, and photo manipulation. He is the owner of Nasu Animator YouTube channel and Nasu Studios.

Who is the creator behind Editor Choice?

Editor Choice is the brainchild of Naman Jain, also known as Nasu. Naman is a computer science student with a passion for various creative pursuits, including blender animation, graphic design, and photo manipulation. He is the owner of Nasu Animator YouTube channel and Nasu Studios. With his expertise in the field, Naman recognized the need for a platform dedicated to editors and their requirements. Thus, Editor Choice was born. Naman's diverse background, coupled with his freelancing work for Indian and international YouTubers, and his love for gaming, particularly Minecraft, brings a unique perspective and understanding to the curation of the apps and services offered on Editor Choice.
